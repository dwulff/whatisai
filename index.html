What do you think about AI?

Welcome, and thank you for participating! With this 3 min survey, we want to find out how you think about artificial intelligence (AI). We will ask you to list five things that come to mind, when you think about artificial intelligence, and to answer some demographic questions. Participation is voluntary and completely anonymous. The resulting data will be used for a scientific research project and later shared with the public. By clicking the "Start" button you confirm that you are at least 14 years old and consent to the collection, use, and sharing of your data.
